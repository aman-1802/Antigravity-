## Spotlight: 🧠 China Pursues Self-Improving AI
- **Link**: [🧠 China Pursues Self-Improving AI](https://www.scmp.com/tech/tech-war/article/3356788/china-races-against-us-ais-holy-grail-self-improving-tech?utm_source=rss_feed)

The global race for Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) has entered its most volatile phase as both US and Chinese laboratories attempt to achieve recursive self-improvement (RSI). Long considered the holy grail of computer science, RSI allows an AI model to analyze, rewrite, and optimize its own underlying algorithms without human intervention, potentially triggering an exponential intelligence explosion.

While US giants like Anthropic and OpenAI leverage massive domestic compute cluster advantages, Chinese firms and academic institutions are optimizing algorithmic efficiencies and relying on hyper-refined synthetic training data to bypass hardware limitations caused by Washington's semiconductor export restrictions.

The country or company that successfully implements a stable, self-improving recursive loop first will gain an compounding technological advantage, fundamentally reshaping global economic, scientific, and defense landscapes.
